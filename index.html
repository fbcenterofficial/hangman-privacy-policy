Privacy Policy

Effective July 14, 2026Applies to the HangMan mobile app on iOS and Android.

HangMan is a word-guessing game. This page explains, in plain terms, what information the app collects, why, and how you can remove it — because a privacy policy you can't actually read isn't much of one.

1 Information we collect

Creating an account requires an email address, a username, and a password. During play, the app records your score, win/loss record, streak, and premium status so leaderboards, ranks, and the daily-word streak actually work.

AccountEmail, username, password (hashed — see §6)
GameplayScore, wins/losses, streak, daily-word history
PurchasesPremium subscription status
AdvertisingDevice advertising identifier — only with your consent
Not collectedLocation, contacts, photos, microphone

If you use the in-app Contact Us form, it opens your own device's Mail app with your message pre-filled — we only receive it if you choose to send that email yourself.

2 How we use it

  • To create and secure your account, and let you log back in on any device.
  • To track scores, streaks, and ranks, and to run the leaderboard.
  • To unlock and manage premium features (Word Packs, ad removal, catch-up days).
  • To respond to bug reports and feedback you send us directly.
  • To show ads that keep the free version of the app running (see §3).

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for anything beyond running the game and responding to you.

3 Advertising & tracking

Free accounts see ads served by Google AdMob. On iOS, the app asks for App Tracking Transparency permission the first time you open it. If you decline, you'll still see ads — they just won't be personalized using your device's advertising identifier.

Premium subscribers see no ads at all, anywhere in the app.

4 Third-party services

  • Supabase — hosts the database that stores your account and gameplay data. Traffic to it is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).
  • Google AdMob — serves ads and, where permitted, uses advertising identifiers for personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5 Retention & deletion

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account (after confirming your password) — this immediately and permanently removes your account row and locally stored data from our systems. This can't be undone.

6 Security

Passwords are stored using bcrypt, a one-way hash — we cannot read or recover your actual password, only verify it at login. All requests to our backend are made over HTTPS.

7 Children's privacy

HangMan is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us (§10) and we'll remove it.

8 Your rights

You can request a copy of the data tied to your account, or ask us to delete it, by emailing us directly — or by using Delete Account in-app for immediate self-service deletion.

9 Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the update.

10 Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about your data: fbcenterofficial@gmail.com
